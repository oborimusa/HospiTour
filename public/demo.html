Demo Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our demo process.

How long does a demo typically take?

Our demos range from 30-60 minutes depending on your needs and the demo type selected. We'll work with you to find the perfect duration that covers all your questions without taking too much of your time.

Who should attend the demo?

We recommend including decision-makers and key users who will interact with the platform daily. This often includes operations managers, general managers, revenue managers, and IT staff depending on your organization structure.

Is there any cost for the demo?

No, all our demos are completely free with no obligation to purchase. We want you to make an informed decision about whether HospiTour is the right fit for your business.

Can I request specific features to be shown?

Absolutely! Please mention your specific interests in the form so we can tailor the demo accordingly. Our experts will focus on the features most relevant to your business needs.

What if I need to reschedule?

No problem! You'll receive a confirmation email with a link to reschedule your demo up to 2 hours before the scheduled time. We understand that schedules can change.
